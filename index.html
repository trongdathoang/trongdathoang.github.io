I am a Lecturer in Computing Technologies at Swinburne University of Technology. My research focuses on Secure and Trustworthy Machine Learning, Network Security, Privacy-enhanced Technologies, and Encrypted Database systems.

BIO

I earned my Ph.D. in Cybersecurity from Monash University in 2022, an achievement made possible through the invaluable guidance of my supervisors and collaborators, including Prof. Xingliang Yuan (The University of Melbourne), Prof. Shi-Feng Sun (Shanghai Jiao Tong University), Prof. Joseph Liu (Monash University), and Dr. Surya Nepal (Data61, CSIRO).

I had a rewarding experience as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Monash University, the University of Queensland, and CSIRO Data61 from 2022 to mid-2024. My research interests focus on leveraging applied cryptography and machine learning security to address real-world security and privacy challenges in emerging technologies. These include distributed AI-enabled systems, distributed database systems, next-generation wireless networks, on-device machine learning, and confidential computing.
